About Me

Industrious and versatile sophomore at LA's own Loyola Marymount University studying film and screenwriting. Equipped with a widely-ranged skillset, I am a very detail-oriented learner with strong ability to adapt, lead, and communicate.

One Stop Shopping

Comfortable with all facets of the film industry, I have gained experience in all three stages of production and learned how to effectively produce, direct, and edit.

No Time Wasted

While most young film students find it tough to get involved in college, I hit the ground running. While my opportunites to direct were limited, I took on roles like a production designer for an on-campus TV show and I am in the process of writing my own production series which will air on LMU's ROAR Network, the on-campus, student-run television network.

Technologically Fluent

I have mastered all sorts of film-related software and am very fluent with Final Cut Pro, Adobe Premier, Adobe AfterEffects, Avid Media Composer, Celtx, Stop Motion Pro, Apple iMovie, and Adobe Photoshop.

Cursed

Roles: Cinematographer, Actress, Editor, Producer

It was my first time working with a green screen on this Halloween themed production. It took weeks to edit the green screen to create the space scene that these trick-or-treaters get sucked into after eating magical candy. Not only was there experimentation with a green screen, but I also tried to mimic different film genres such as horror and silent film. Check out the video here!
